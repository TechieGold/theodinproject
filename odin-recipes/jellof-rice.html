Jellof rice and chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 3 inch ginger root peeled and sliced into chuncks
  • 4 pounds chicken drumsticks and thighs about 2kg
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoon of chicken bouillon powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 6 large roma tomatoes
  • 16 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 6 cups of Jellof-rice
  • 1/2 cup diced/sliced carrots
  • 1/2 diced/sliced green beans
